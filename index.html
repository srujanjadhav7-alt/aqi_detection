How It Works

Our system uses advanced machine learning to analyze images and detect air quality parameters. The image-based approach captures atmospheric conditions that traditional sensors might miss.

Using a trained Convolutional Neural Network, we process the image through multiple layers of feature extraction, identifying patterns associated with various air quality levels and pollutants.

The result is an AQI reading that corresponds to WHO standards, helping individuals make informed decisions about their health and outdoor activities.
